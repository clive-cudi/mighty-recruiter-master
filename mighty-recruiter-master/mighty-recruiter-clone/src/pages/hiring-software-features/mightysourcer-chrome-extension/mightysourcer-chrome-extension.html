MightySourcer: Find Your Job Candidate’s Contact Information

Find your star job candidate's contact information (like phone and email address) — and add their profile to your MightyRecruiter recruiting software account — in seconds. The MightySourcer Chrome Extension by MightyRecruiter is an extremely easy-to use, free tool for sourcing candidates. Find the right job candidate in 3 steps:

  • Start sourcing the perfect job candidate on job boards like LinkedIn and social media networks like Facebook, Twitter or GitHub
  • Found the one? The MightySourcer Chrome Extension supplies you with their social media profiles, work experience, education, skills, phone number, and email.
  • Send the job candidate an email invitation to apply, right from the extension.
